## LOGLINE

Two space travelers — **Aria** and **Kael** — are separated across the cosmos after their ship shatters in a wormhole collapse. Guided by a mysterious resonance that pulses in sync with their heartbeats, they navigate parallel journeys through nebulae, asteroid fields, and alien worlds to find each other at the edge of the observable universe.

## VISUAL DIRECTION NOTES

### Cinematography
- Anamorphic widescreen (2.39:1 native, cropped for platform)
- Slow motion dominant (48fps rendered at 24fps)
- Lens flares used sparingly — only on signal pulses and the reunion moment
- Depth of field: extremely shallow for intimate moments, deep for cosmic landscapes

### Color Grading
- Overall: cool shadows (blue-violet), warm highlights (gold-amber)
- Act I: dominated by violet and cyan — isolation, cold beauty
- Act II: warm tones creep in — gold, magenta — hope emerging
- Act III: full warmth — gold, amber, white — resolution and unity

### VFX Requirements
- Zero-gravity cloth simulation (Aria's dress)
- Particle systems: crystal debris, ring dust, comet trail, starfield
- Wormhole tunnel effect (procedural spiral)
- Supernova explosion (volumetric rendering)
- Holographic transparency effect (dream dance scene)
- Light-pulse chest glow (tracked to actor)

### Transitions
- Act I→II: Signal light expands to fill frame, resolves into moon surface
- Scene-to-scene: Beat-synced cuts on downbeats, dissolves on sustained notes
- Final shot: Slow iris-out to single point of light
